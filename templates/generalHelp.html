Leave of Absence

The Leave of Absence application procedure is administered by the Faculty Manager's office
The point of contact for applications is the Faculty of science manager (fmscience@uct.ac.za)
Complete an Undergraduate Leave of Absence Application (ACA37): https://forms.uct.ac.za/studentforms.htm#aca37.
For more details, click on the guide below:
http://www.sss.uct.ac.za/sites/default/files/image_tool/images/152/Leave%20of%20Absence%20Service%20Request%20Submission.pdf

Longterm/ongoing personal problem: BSc students can get help from Cecile.Reed@uct.ac.za
Problems understanding lecture content: Email the senior tutor, let's call her SNRTUT001@myuct.ac.za for now
Poor assignment marks: go to your prac session in the lab and ask tutors, or go to CS Hotseat
